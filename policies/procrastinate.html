Tomorrow-Me Procrastination Insurance

We insure your "I'll do it tomorrow." (Tomorrow never comes.)
Policy No. HLI-LAZY-PRO-002 Monthly Premium KRW 4,900 Refundable Deposit KRW 20,000

Article 1 (Purpose)

This Policy sets out the rights and obligations of the contract for the "Tomorrow-Me Procrastination Insurance", whereby the Insurer indemnifies the small everyday losses arising from the Insured's laziness and encourages behavioral improvement.

Article 2 (Definitions)

#TermDefinition
1"Goal"A single task registered at enrollment (e.g., submitting documents, exercising, cleaning).
2"Procrastination Detection"A state verified as incomplete up to 24 hours before the Goal deadline.
3"Completion"Submission of completion verification within the Goal deadline.

Article 3 (Insuring Agreement & Benefits)

Insured EventBenefit Payable
Upon Procrastination DetectionUp to five (5) reminders.
Upon deadline overrunConsolation Points of 2,000 P.
Upon CompletionFull deposit refund plus an Achievement Badge.

Article 4 (Premium)

The monthly premium is KRW 4,900, payable automatically each month. Delay in premium payment is itself a form of laziness covered hereunder; however, the contract may be automatically terminated upon two or more consecutive delinquencies.

Article 5 (Refundable Deposit)

A refundable deposit of KRW 20,000 applies. It is refunded in full upon verified Completion within the deadline; otherwise it is forfeited to the 'Tomorrow-Me Fund'.

Article 6 (Exclusions — Events Not Covered)

  1. 'Almost done' shall not be recognized as Completion.
  2. Self-extension of the deadline is excluded.
  3. Blocking reminder notifications voids coverage.
  4. Goals such as 'resting' or 'doing nothing' are ineligible for enrollment.

Article 7 (Claims)

The Insured shall claim benefits (consolation points, etc.) through the application within thirty (30) days of the occurrence. Where the claim period lapses due to procrastination, the right to claim shall be extinguished, which is deemed consistent with the purpose of this Policy.

Article 8 (Cancellation & Withdrawal)

The Insured may withdraw the application within fifteen (15) days of subscription. Where the Insured is "too lazy even to cancel," the contract shall automatically renew, and the resulting premium payment shall be deemed the voluntary intent of the Insured.

Article 9 (Illustration)

The Insured's Goal was 'submit résumé' but repeated 'I'll do it tomorrow' three times and failed to complete. Consolation Points of 2,000 P are paid, the deposit is forfeited, and a free retry voucher is auto-issued, extending the subscription.
