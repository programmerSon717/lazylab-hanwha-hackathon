Article 1 (Purpose)

This Policy sets out the rights and obligations of the contract for the "Resolution-Breaker Self-Development Insurance", whereby the Insurer indemnifies the small everyday losses arising from the Insured's laziness and encourages behavioral improvement.

Article 2 (Definitions)

#TermDefinition
1"Study Verification"Completion of one lecture or a 30-minute focused-study record.
2"Course Completion"100% completion of the enrolled course.
3"Memorization Goal"A daily word or volume target.

Article 3 (Insuring Agreement & Benefits)

Insured EventBenefit Payable
Upon a 3-day study lapseA progress reminder plus a mentor message.
Upon falling short of the GoalConsolation Points of 2,000 P.
Upon Course Completion / Memorization Goal achievedFull deposit refund plus a Completion Badge.

Article 4 (Premium)

The monthly premium is KRW 5,900, payable automatically each month. Delay in premium payment is itself a form of laziness covered hereunder; however, the contract may be automatically terminated upon two or more consecutive delinquencies.

Article 5 (Refundable Deposit)

A refundable deposit of KRW 30,000 applies, refunded in full upon achieving the target study volume; otherwise not refunded.

Article 6 (Exclusions — Events Not Covered)

  1. Merely 'leaving a lecture playing' at 4x speed is not recognized as study.
  2. Merely purchasing a textbook (unboxing) is not covered.
  3. 'Getting inspired' may not be reported as study.
  4. Failure of last-minute cramming the night before an exam is excluded as the Insured's own responsibility.

Article 7 (Claims)

The Insured shall claim benefits (consolation points, etc.) through the application within thirty (30) days of the occurrence. Where the claim period lapses due to procrastination, the right to claim shall be extinguished, which is deemed consistent with the purpose of this Policy.

Article 8 (Cancellation & Withdrawal)

The Insured may withdraw the application within fifteen (15) days of subscription. Where the Insured is "too lazy even to cancel," the contract shall automatically renew, and the resulting premium payment shall be deemed the voluntary intent of the Insured.

Article 9 (Illustration)

The Insured enrolled with a Goal of memorizing 30 English words daily but 'kept trying to memorize and kept forgetting,' failing verification for three consecutive days. Consolation Points of 2,000 P are paid; the KRW 30,000 deposit is not refunded.
