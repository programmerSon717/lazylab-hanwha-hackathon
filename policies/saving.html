Empty-Wallet Defense Insurance

Empty wallet again this month? We insure impulse buys.
Policy No. HLI-LAZY-SAV-004 Monthly Premium KRW 4,900 Refundable Deposit KRW 50,000

Article 1 (Purpose)

This Policy sets out the rights and obligations of the contract for the "Empty-Wallet Defense Insurance", whereby the Insurer indemnifies the small everyday losses arising from the Insured's laziness and encourages behavioral improvement.

Article 2 (Definitions)

#TermDefinition
1"Impulse Purchase"A single late-night (23:00–03:00) transaction exceeding KRW 50,000.
2"Savings Goal"The target savings amount registered at the start of the month.
3"Empty Wallet"A month-end balance below target.

Article 3 (Insuring Agreement & Benefits)

Insured EventBenefit Payable
Upon a late-night payment attemptA 'Do you really need this?' warning alert.
Upon failing the Savings GoalConsolation Points of 3,000 P.
Upon achieving the monthly Savings GoalFull deposit refund plus preferential interest.

Article 4 (Premium)

The monthly premium is KRW 4,900, payable automatically each month. Delay in premium payment is itself a form of laziness covered hereunder; however, the contract may be automatically terminated upon two or more consecutive delinquencies.

Article 5 (Refundable Deposit)

A refundable deposit of KRW 50,000 applies, refunded in full upon achieving the monthly Savings Goal; otherwise forfeited, and any investment returns earned on the deposit during the accrual period accrue to the Insurer.

Article 6 (Exclusions — Events Not Covered)

  1. 'Stress shopping' constitutes grounds for exclusion.
  2. Items merely placed in a cart are not covered (no purchase).
  3. A payment made after the warning alert is deemed willful and the consolation amount is reduced by 50%.
  4. Investment and crypto losses are not covered.

Article 7 (Claims)

The Insured shall claim benefits (consolation points, etc.) through the application within thirty (30) days of the occurrence. Where the claim period lapses due to procrastination, the right to claim shall be extinguished, which is deemed consistent with the purpose of this Policy.

Article 8 (Cancellation & Withdrawal)

The Insured may withdraw the application within fifteen (15) days of subscription. Where the Insured is "too lazy even to cancel," the contract shall automatically renew, and the resulting premium payment shall be deemed the voluntary intent of the Insured.

The Insured targeted KRW 300,000 in savings but fell short due to two late-night Impulse Purchases. Consolation Points of 3,000 P are paid; the KRW 50,000 deposit is not refunded.
