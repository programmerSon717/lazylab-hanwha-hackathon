Article 1 (Purpose)

This Policy sets out the rights and obligations of the contract for the "Comprehensive Laziness Insurance", whereby the Insurer indemnifies the small everyday losses arising from the Insured's laziness and encourages behavioral improvement.

Article 2 (Definitions)

#TermDefinition
1"Goal"Any resolution or task across all categories registered by the Insured.
2"Achievement"Verified completion of at least one of the monthly registered Goals.

Article 3 (Insuring Agreement & Benefits)

Insured EventBenefit Payable
Upon detecting any 'I'll do it later'Category-specific reminders.

Article 4 (Premium)

The monthly premium is KRW 6,900, payable automatically each month. Delay in premium payment is itself a form of laziness covered hereunder; however, the contract may be automatically terminated upon two or more consecutive delinquencies.

Article 5 (Refundable Deposit)

A refundable deposit of KRW 30,000 applies, refunded in full upon achieving at least one monthly Goal; if zero Goals are achieved, it is not refunded.

Article 6 (Exclusions — Events Not Covered)

  1. All exclusions of the individual policies enumerated herein apply mutatis mutandis.
  2. The mere claim that 'starting is half the battle' is not recognized as Achievement.
  3. Registering Goals without attempting a single one is excluded.

Article 7 (Claims)

The Insured shall claim benefits (consolation points, etc.) through the application within thirty (30) days of the occurrence. Where the claim period lapses due to procrastination, the right to claim shall be extinguished, which is deemed consistent with the purpose of this Policy.

Article 8 (Cancellation & Withdrawal)

The Insured may withdraw the application within fifteen (15) days of subscription. Where the Insured is "too lazy even to cancel," the contract shall automatically renew, and the resulting premium payment shall be deemed the voluntary intent of the Insured.

Article 9 (Illustration)

The Insured registered three Goals (exercise, savings, study) but verified none by month-end. Consolation Points are paid; the KRW 30,000 deposit is not refunded.
